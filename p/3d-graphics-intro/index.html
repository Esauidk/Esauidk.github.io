Overview & Affine Transformation

3D Rotation

  • Cos(theta) in the matrix scaling spots
  • Sin(theta) and -Sin(theta) in the matrix sheering spots

2D Rotation $$ \begin{bmatrix} Cos(ϴ) & -Sin(ϴ) \ Sin(ϴ) & Cos(ϴ) \end{bmatrix}$$

3D Rotation $$ \begin{bmatrix} Cos(ϴ) & -Sin(ϴ) & 0 \ Sin(ϴ) & Cos(ϴ) & 0 \ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$

Homogeneous Coordinates
